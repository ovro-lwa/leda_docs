OVRO Observing Windows

An observing window can start 1 hour after sunset, and after the galaxy has set and advanced 20° below the horizon. It finishes at sunrise or galaxy rise. In the table, "Galaxy" means the Galactic centre. Sunset+1h means one hour after sunset, and Galaxy Set-20° means the galaxy has set and dropped 20° below the horizon. The window length is the length of time that is available for an observing window given the above rules.
